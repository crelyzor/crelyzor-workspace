## Design Principles (Both Decks)

- **Dark background** — feels premium, modern, tech-forward
- **Max 30 words per slide** outside of tables/lists
- **One idea per slide** — never two messages competing
- **Real screenshots** — no mockups, no stock photos
- **Consistent type:** one sans-serif (Inter or Geist), two weights max
- **No bullet soup** — if you have more than 4 bullets, redesign the slide

---

## What NOT to Put In These Decks

- No competitor comparison matrix (weakens you, invites debate)
- No "we are X for Y" analogies (reductive)
- No vague TAM methodology (show the math clearly or don't show it)
- No team slide filler like "passionate about productivity" — use facts
- No future features list on the customer deck (confuses the product story)
